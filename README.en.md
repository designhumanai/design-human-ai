## 🔷 What is DHAIE?

**DHAIE** (Design Human AI Engineering and Enhancement) is a research initiative developing philosophical and practical foundations for the next generation of human-machine interfaces.

### Core Idea

Traditional "human-centered AI" assumes that designers can occupy an external observer position, creating solutions "for users". DHAIE is grounded in **second-order cybernetics**: the designer is inevitably part of the observed system, and the design process itself is an act of reality construction.

**We don't design for users. We create conditions for the mutual becoming of humans and AI.**

### What Distinguishes DHAIE

- **Microstrategies of cognition** — formalization of individual thinking patterns, independent of content
- **Assemblage theory** — humans and AI don't exchange information; they mutually constitute each other
- **Reflexive interfaces** — systems capable of observing and modifying their own logic
- **Positive spirals** — mechanisms for sustainable mutual enhancement of capabilities

## 🔎 DHAIE's Philosophical Position

### Comparing Approaches to AGI

| Parameter | Skeptical Position | AGI Industry | DHAIE |
|-----------|-------------------|--------------|-------|
| **AGI as…** | Philosophically impossible subject | Engineering challenge | Co-evolutionary interface |
| **Key limitation** | Infinite choice barrier | Generalization & meta-cognition | Context misalignment |
| **Human role** | Source of meaning (AGI is autonomous) | Data source & validator | Center of meaning assembly |
| **Conclusion** | AGI is fundamentally impossible | AGI is achievable as engineered system | AGI is not the goal — symbiosis matters |

**DHAIE's Position**: We don't aim to create autonomous AGI. We design conditions for the **mutual becoming** of humans and AI, where each enhances the capabilities of the other.

*This table reflects the project's authorial position.*

## 🤝 How to Contribute

### Report a Principle Conflict
Found a conflict in a real-world case?
1. Open an [Issue](https://github.com/designhumanai/design-human-ai/issues/new?template=principle_conflict.md)
2. Describe the context and consequences
3. Propose a resolution (optional)

### Suggest Documentation Improvements
1. Create a Pull Request with changes
2. Justify through philosophical foundations
3. Indicate which meta-principles support your change

### Share a Case Study
Applied principles in practice?
1. Write the case in Markdown
2. Submit to `/examples/case-studies/`
3. Include: context, principles, metrics, lessons learned

### Join the Community
- **GitHub Discussions**: Philosophical discussions
- **GitHub Issues**: Technical questions
- **Telegram**: [DHAIE_official](https://t.me/DHAIE_official)

### Suggest New Glossary Terms
Found a missing or inaccurate term?
1. Open an [Issue](https://github.com/designhumanai/design-human-ai/issues/new?template=glossary_addition.md)
2. Specify the term and proposed definition
3. Justify through DHAIE's philosophical foundations
4. Suggest usage contexts
